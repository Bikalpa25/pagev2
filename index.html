Regen: Trash to Treasure

Regen nepal pvt. Ltd. is a company determined to utilizing waste plastics materials into products like plastic sheets, Beams and 3D printing filaments. Recycled plastic sheets can be used in desks, benches, tables with replacement of wooden or plywood sheets in a comparative pricing. Extruded plastic beams can be made into any cross section and can be used as a frame or support structure. We also produce 3d printing filaments from recycled PLA plastics as well as virgin PLA pellets.

Condition Monitoring Sytem Implementing IOT and ML in nepalese Manufacturing industries

This project develops and evaluates a real-time condition monitoring system using IoT and machine learning to improve operational efficiency and maintenance in Nepalese manufacturing. Key industries and machinery were identified, appropriate sensors selected, and an IoT-based network was designed for real-time data collection. ESP32 and IoT configuration facilitated data acquisition of current and vibration, identifying potential anomalies or degradation trends. The system was tested with CGIP, Nawalparasi, and Yeti Plastic Industries, Kavre.
